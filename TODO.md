- [x] Number of patients on caseload: total number of patients who have been referred, but not discharged, by end of reporting month
  - Number of patients whose referral date is before the end of the reporting month
  - This does include patients whose referral date is before the start of the report month!

- [x] Rename "Number of DNA" to "Number of DNA contacts"
- [x] "Number of patients receiving psychological therapies": number of patients who had their initial assessment during the reporting month, and are diagnosed positive
- [x] "Number of patients diagnosed with ADHD with new diagnosis": number of patients with a positive diagnosis and no pre-existing diagnosis out of all patients who had their initial assessment during the reporting month
- [x] "Percentage of patients diagnosed with ADHD of new diagnosis referrals": percentage of patients with a positive diagnosis out of all patients who had their initial assessment during the reporting month and who did not have a pre-existing diagnosis
- [x] "Number of patients prescribed medication under Shared Care": Total number of patients on caseload who are on medication and are on Shared Care (requires Shared Care in Cliniko)
